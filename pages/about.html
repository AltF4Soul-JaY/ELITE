Information

EL1TE eSports is a leading Indian organization established in 2018. Initially formed as a classic PUBG MOBILE clan, it transitioned into a full-fledged eSports organization on Discord in 2020.

Aim

Our mission is to empower aspiring players and underdogs by providing a platform to showcase their talent and achieve excellence on a larger stage. Through major prize pool tournaments and competitive Tier 3, Tier 2, and Semi-Tier 1 scrims, we strive to nurture emerging talent and contribute to the growth of the eSports community.
